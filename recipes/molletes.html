Molletes

Description

Molletes are a traditional Mexican open sandwich with refried beans and melted cheese served with salsa. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or as a tasty snack.

Ingredients

  • bolillo rolls
  • butter
  • refried beans
  • cheddar cheese
  • salsa pico de gallo

Steps

  1. Slice the bollillo in half, lengthwise.
  2. Spread butter on each half.
  3. Spread refried beans on bread. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the beans.
  4. Bake the molletes in preheated oven until the cheese is melted.
  5. Top the molletes with salsa pico de gallo.
